About the song

Khaterate To by Minoo Lakani

The dedicated page for خاطرات تو brings together the original Persian title, Latin transliteration, English meaning, artwork, and release link. This structure helps listeners locate the song even when they remember only one version of its title.

Memories can keep a person close long after circumstances change. The title “Your Memories” gives attention not only to the past itself, but also to the emotional life those remembered moments continue to carry.
