Approved Short Biography

Short bio

Minoo Lakani is an independent Persian and English songwriter, music artist, producer, and storyteller creating contemporary original music centered on emotion, memory, love, resilience, and human connection. Through Minoo Lakani Production, she brings songwriting, production direction, visual identity, and official release presentation together in one growing bilingual catalog.

Approved Long Biography

Long bio

Minoo Lakani is an independent songwriter, music artist, producer, and storyteller working in both Persian and English. Her songs are shaped by direct emotional language and contemporary production, with recurring themes of love, memory, identity, resilience, longing, and the lasting impact of human relationships.

Her bilingual catalog combines Persian-language songwriting with English releases and presents each song through a consistent official identity. Persian titles are supported by Latin transliterations and English meanings so listeners can locate the work across languages and search platforms. English releases preserve the same intimate and emotionally immediate approach.

Through Minoo Lakani Production, she oversees the creative direction surrounding the music, including song concepts, production choices, artwork, catalog organization, and official web presentation. Her website serves as the authoritative source for biography, discography, press information, news, media, and official listening destinations.
