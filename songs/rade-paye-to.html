About the song

Rade Paye To by Minoo Lakani

This official page gives رد پای تو a permanent place within Minoo Lakani’s catalog. It combines the Persian title, Latin transliteration, title meaning, artwork, and official listening destination so the release can be found through several types of search.

A footprint is evidence that someone passed through a place. Used emotionally, it becomes a symbol of memory, influence, and the lasting marks left by a meaningful relationship.
