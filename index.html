Official Biography

About Minoo Lakani

Minoo Lakani is an independent Persian and English songwriter, music artist, producer, and storyteller whose work is built around emotional clarity, memorable language, and contemporary musical production.

Creative Profile

Music from memory, emotion, and lived experience

Minoo Lakani writes original songs in Persian and English. Her catalog moves between intimate pop, emotionally driven ballads, contemporary production, and cinematic storytelling. The recurring focus is direct human feeling: love, memory, resilience, longing, identity, and the emotional traces people leave behind.

Her creative process brings songwriting, concept development, production direction, visual presentation, and release strategy together under one independent identity. Each official release is presented with clear artwork, language information, and a dedicated song page so listeners can discover the work in Persian, English, or transliterated form.

Minoo Lakani Production serves as the official home for this growing catalog and for future music, visual stories, collaborations, and media information.
