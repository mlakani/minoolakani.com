About the song

Ghashangtarin Dalil by Minoo Lakani

This official song page presents قشنگترین دلیل with its Persian spelling, transliteration, English meaning, artwork, and release link. It creates a clear reference point for listeners searching by either the original title or its Latin spelling.

The title turns the idea of a “reason” into something emotional rather than logical. It suggests that love, memory, or another person can become the most beautiful explanation for hope and perseverance.
